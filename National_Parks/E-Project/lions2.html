About Sandiego Zoo Park

What is Sandiego Park Zoo ? and where is located established?


The San Diego Zoo is one of the most famous and largest zoos in the world, known for its extensive collection of animals and commitment to wildlife conservation. The zoo is situated in Balboa Park, in the city of San Diego, California, USA.The San Diego Zoo was officially founded in 1916, following the Panama-California Exposition held in 1915–1916.




Opening & Closed Timings

The zoo is typically open every day from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

During peak seasons (like summer and holidays), the zoo may extend its hours It’s always a good idea to check the official website for the most current timings.

Animal shows and encounters are scheduled throughout the day, with times varying based on season and animal activity. The zoo’s schedule for animal experiences is available at the entrance or online Family-Friendly Features.



The zoo offers family-oriented programs and camps for kids, including day camps, overnight camps, and educational programs. These programs allow children to engage in fun and educational experiences related to animals, conservation, and the natural world.






Animals List

The San Diego Zoo is home to over 12,000 animals, representing more than 650 species and subspecies. The zoo is renowned for its diversity of wildlife, featuring animals from around the world, including iconic species such as:
