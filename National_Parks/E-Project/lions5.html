About Marwell Zoo Park

What is Marwell Park Zoo ? and where is located established?


Marwell Zoo, located in Colden Common near Winchester, Hampshire, England, is a 140-acre park home to over 1,200 animals representing 149 species. Established in 1972 by John Knowles, it is operated by Marwell Wildlife, a registered charity dedicated to wildlife conservation.




Opening & Closed Timings

The zoo opens daily at 10:00 AM To 8:00 PM.

Last admission is 90 minutes before closing, and animal houses begin to close 30 minutes before the park closes.

Marwell Zoo hosts various events throughout the year, including the "Glow Marwell" light experience during the winter season.



While there are no specific "Ladies' Days," the zoo offers family fun days and other activities suitable for all visitors.
