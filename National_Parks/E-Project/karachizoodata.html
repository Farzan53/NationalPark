About Karachi Zoo Park

What is Karachi Park Zoo ? and where is located established?


The Karachi Zoo is one of the oldest and most well-known zoos in Karachi, Pakistan. It is located in the city center and serves as a major recreational and educational spot for residents and visitors Karachi Zoo, also known as Gulshan-e-Iqbal Zoo, is located at Ayub National Park, Karachi, Pakistan. The zoo is situated in a central area of the city, making it easily accessible for locals and tourists alike The Karachi Zoo was officially established in 1878, making it one of the oldest zoos in Pakistan.
