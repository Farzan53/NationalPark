



ZSL London Zoo, located in Regent's Park, London, is the world's oldest scientific zoo, established in 1828. It is managed by the Zoological Society of London (ZSL), a charity dedicated to worldwide wildlife conservation.

**Animals and Exhibits:**

The zoo is home to over 20,000 animals representing more than 750 species. Notable exhibits include:

- **Land of the Lions:** An immersive exhibit replicating the Sasan Gir in Gujarat, India.

- **Penguin Beach:** The UK's largest penguin pool.

- **Tiger Territory:** Home to Sumatran tigers.

- **Gorilla Kingdom:** An African rainforest environment housing western lowland gorillas.

- **Reptile House:** Featuring a variety of reptiles, including snakes, lizards, and crocodiles.
