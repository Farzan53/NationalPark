

Marwell Zoo, located in Colden Common near Winchester, Hampshire, England, is a 140-acre park home to over 1,200 animals representing 149 species. Established in 1972 by John Knowles, it is operated by Marwell Wildlife, a registered charity dedicated to wildlife conservation. 

**Opening Hours:**

Marwell Zoo is open year-round, except on Christmas Day and Boxing Day. The zoo opens daily at 10:00 AM, with closing times varying seasonally:

- **Glow Marwell Event (29 Nov – 23 Dec / 27 Dec – 30 Dec / 4 – 5 Jan):** Open from 1:00 PM to 8:00 PM.

- **1 January 2025:** Open from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

- **2 January 2025 – 3 January 2025:** Open from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

- **4 January 2025 – 5 January 2025:** Open from 1:00 PM to 4:00 PM.

- **6 January 2025 – 31 March 2025:** Open from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

- **1 April 2025 – 30 September 2025:** Open from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

- **1 October 2025 – 31 October 2025:** Open from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

- **1 November 2025 – 31 December 2025:** Open from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

Last admission is 90 minutes before closing, and animal houses begin to close 30 minutes before the park closes. 

**Special Events:**

Marwell Zoo hosts various events throughout the year, including the "Glow Marwell" light experience during the winter season. While there are no specific "Ladies' Days," the zoo offers family fun days and other activities suitable for all visitors. 

**Animals and Exhibits:**

The zoo features a diverse range of exhibits, including:

- **Energy for Life: Tropical House:** An immersive environment with tropical plants and free-flying birds.

- **Wallaby Walkthrough:** A walkthrough exhibit allowing close encounters with wallabies.

- **Penguin Cove:** Home to Humboldt penguins.

- **Lemur Loop:** A walkthrough exhibit featuring various lemur species.

- **Adventure Playgrounds:** Five play areas designed for children of all ages.
